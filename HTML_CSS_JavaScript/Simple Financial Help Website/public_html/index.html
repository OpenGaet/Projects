How It Works

This is a very simple calculator. It assumes a retirement age of 65. Therefore you can only enter an age between 0 - 64. However, you can try to save up to 100 million dollars if you wish.

It calculates two different savings options, investing in the stock market or simply putting your money in a savings account at a bank. The bank account will not accrue any interest and will actually lose money due to inflation. The rate of growth used for the stock investment is 5.0%. This number is based on the historical compounded annual growth rate, minus inflation, of the U.S. stock market.

The amounts returned will tell you how much you would need to invest or save per year in order to reach the amount you entered by the retirement age.
